General Assessment/Evaluation

This is a screening questionnaire used to identify potential mental health issues in individuals. It is a self-report questionnaire consisting of 14 questions, each with four response options. Based on the results user will be directed to disorder specific questions. The questions are based on GHQ12 and the DSM (Diagnostic and Statiscal Manual of Mental Disorders)
