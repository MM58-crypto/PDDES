About PDDES

Greetings, welcome to PDDES (short for Psychological Disorders Diagnosis Expert System), An online expert system for diagnosing/predicting psychological disorders. This online system was built using html, css, javascript and django. Since it is an expert system, it is composed of three main components: an inference engine, a knowledge base and of course a user interface. Moreover, this system aims to assist those might be suffering from psychological issues as well as experts in the domain of psychology/psychiatry to facilitate the diagnosis process. Not to mention, other than to help or atleast slightly guide those who are struggling with psychological issues, this website serves as my final year project.
